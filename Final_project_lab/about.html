Meet the Team

We are Steve and Glenda Pearson of Chattanooga, Tennessee. Steve has been a minister for over four decades and has most recently served with the Tennessee Baptist Mission Board. He has specialized in all areas of helping churches from evangelism, small groups, resourcing and more. He and Glenda have been married for over 40 years and have ministered together in several churches. Glenda also served within guest services, as well as being on staff to handle first impressions for a large church. They are equipped to help guide you in understanding the different aspects of meeting the needs of your first time guests, as well as the needs of your church family. Contact them today to see how they can walk you through the guest services process.
